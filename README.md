# Solar Rooftop Market Entry Strategy — India (C&I Segment)

A complete consulting-style strategy project covering market research, structured problem-solving, and a 3-year market entry plan for the Commercial & Industrial (C&I) rooftop solar sector in India.

---

## 1. Project Overview

This project develops a full **market entry strategy** for a mid-scale solar EPC company entering the **Commercial & Industrial (C&I)** rooftop market in India. It includes:

- Market research  
- Strategic frameworks (Issue Tree, Hypothesis Tree, MECE, SWOT)  
- Market entry strategy with a 3-year roadmap  
- A 12-slide consulting-style deck  
- Power BI data analysis and visualizations  

The project demonstrates structured thinking, data analysis, and strategy formulation similar to consulting workflows.
